Option B — Status-First Cards

Each card answers "does this truck need my attention right now?" at a glance — a coloured top stripe (calendar colour), pills for any active issue (oil, sticker, blocked, maintenance), and the absolute minimum data underneath. Click a card to open a full-detail modal with everything (blocked-days timeline, maintenance, history, edit). The bulky inline "block days" form moves into the modal.
